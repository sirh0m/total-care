PAYMENT CARD AND/OR PAYMENT ACCOUNT ACQUIRING AND SECURITY

Payments for products purchased may be made by the following means:

  • Online Payment (Using bank card)
  • Cash deposits
  • Speed Point Payments (Head-office or Stockists)
  • Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT)

Once payment is made by a Member, Proof of Payment must be sent to Total Care Europe Unipessoal LDA finance department; via email or WhatsApp, and the Member is obliged to ensure that the Total Care Europe Unipessoal LDA finance department has been notified of the payment, and that the finances department has received said Proof Of Payment.

Payment is recognized by Total Care Europe Unipessoal LDA once the funds are reflected in the company’s bank account

The date on which the funds for ‘First Purchase’ are reflected in the company’s bank account and the order marked as paid on the system is the date on which the distributor will be considered active.

Stock can be collected or delivered once payment reflects to the company’s bank account.

Distributors are to allow 48 hours for collection and up to 72 hours for delivery over & above 5 working days nationwide or 15 business days for international members.

Level based Commissions are payable by the 10th of each month to a distributor’s bank account or afterwards if the 10th falls on a weekend.

Payments will not be made to 3rd parties, unless there is prior written arrangement accepted and approved by Total Care Europe Unipessoal LDA.

Level based commissions will be paid to an active distributor for the preceding month’s volume of business, for the month in question.

Rank bonuses will be paid once off within 60 days of qualifying. Incentives are awarded 90 days after the Annual Total Care Europe Unipessoal LDA Annual Event.

Monthly volume bonus will be paid out by the 15th of the following month. Annual Loyalty bonuses will be paid annually within 90 days after the annual event.

Distributors may verify their commissions, incentives and bonuses by accessing their back office on the Total Care Europe Unipessoal LDA system using their login credentials.

The Total Care Europe Unipessoal LDA system is an online system and distributors have unlimited access to the system 24/7

Total Care Europe Unipessoal LDA Members are to ensure that their accurate banking details have been uploaded onto the Total Care Europe Unipessoal LDA It is also the possibility of each Member to ensure that their banking details are updated on the system, should any details change.
